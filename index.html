The Tide

The Tide is a Wednesday, publication that focuses on local, national and international issues of wide-scope importance. Its mission for the community is to promote a publication devoted to pure, honest journalism that allows members from the Rocky Hill community to express their opinions without fear of subjugation.

The Hill

We are a biannually published literary and art review with a unique focus on aesthetics and writing of all sorts. Here we offer student writers, thinkers, and artists of Rocky Hill School a common platform for publishing their original work. Contribute to our ongoing dialogue about all facets of life, be they economic or existential.

The Tempest

The Tempest is a biweekly blog platform created and led by students at Rocky Hill School. We hope to give prospective families an accurate portrayal of student life as students share their experiences from their time here. We encourage you to explore our content and see what Rocky Hill is really like. Watch our students Pursue their Passion.
